About Me

I am currently a second year PhD student in Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). My academic path began with a B.Sc. in Statistics from the University of Calcutta, followed by an M.Sc. in Statistics from IIT Kanpur. Currently, I am working under the supervision of Dr. Snigdhanshu(Ansu) Chatterjee on the equation discovery of stochastic dynamical systems.efore joining UMBC, I earned my Master of Science (M.Sc) in Statistics from the Department of Mathematics & Statistics, Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. During my time at IIT Kanpur, I gained research experience working with Dr. Dootika Vats. Precedingly, I obtained my Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Statistics Honours from the University of Calcutta.
