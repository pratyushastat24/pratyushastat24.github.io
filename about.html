About Me

I am a second-year Doctoral student in Statistics at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC)[cite: 4, 5, 6]. Currently, I am working under the supervision of Dr. Anindya (Ansu) Roy and Dr. Snigdhanshu Chatterjee, focusing on the equation discovery of stochastic dynamical systems.

Before joining UMBC, I earned my Master of Science (M.Sc) in Statistics from the Department of Mathematics & Statistics, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kanpur[cite: 8, 9]. During my time at IIT Kanpur, I gained significant research experience working under the guidance of Dr. Dootika Vats, where I focused on Bayesian Phylogenetic Tree inference and high-dimensional prediction models[cite: 47, 53]. Precedingly, I obtained my Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Statistics Honours from the University of Calcutta[cite: 11].
