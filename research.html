Research Highlights

Equation Discovery

Developing a framework using Local Polynomial Regression to recover hidden differential laws from noisy, dynamical systems.

Collaborators: Dr. Snigdhanshu Chatterjee, Dr Siddhartha Nandy

Phylogenetic Inference

Perform MCMC algorithms to reconstruct evolutionary lineages and assess model sensitivity within complex biological spaces.

Collaborator: Dr. Dootika Vats (IIT Kanpur)

Predictive Stability

Implementing a novel mixture estimator for LOO-CV that ensures finite variance and robust out-of-sample accuracy for Bayesian predictive density.

Collaborator: Dr. Dootika Vats (IIT Kanpur)
